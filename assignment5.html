Challenge 5: Accessibility Concerns

Tables have major accesibility concerns. They are suggested to be used for numeric reasons only. Using a table for presentation divides the content into sections that is random and doesn't relate to the content. Non-traditional devices are not able to render this information in the table or display it correctly. This ends up ruining the accesibility for the others. As we have been taught, it is best to stay away from tables.
